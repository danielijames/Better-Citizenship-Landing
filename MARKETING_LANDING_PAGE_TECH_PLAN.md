# Better Citizenship — Marketing Landing Page Tech Plan

**Purpose:** This document provides everything another model needs to create a beautiful, accurate marketing landing page for the Better Citizenship mobile app. Follow this plan to build a landing page that reflects the app's capabilities, values, design, and target audience.

---

## 1. Product Overview

**App Name:** Better Citizenship

**Tagline (suggested):** *"The education needed to blur borders starts here."*

**One-liner:** A mobile study app that helps people prepare for citizenship tests in the US, Canada, UK, Australia, France, and Spain—in their preferred language with multiple study modes and an AI-powered search assistant.

---

## 2. Target Customer

**Primary users:**
- People preparing for citizenship/naturalization tests in any of the 6 supported countries
- Immigrants and aspiring citizens who want to study in their native language (English, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese)
- Learners who benefit from flashcards, timed quizzes, or randomized practice
- Anyone who wants a supportive, human-centered tool to help them pass their citizenship exam

**Customer pain points:**
- Citizenship tests are intimidating; official study materials can feel overwhelming
- Limited study options in native languages
- Need flexible study modes (quick sprints vs. deep review)
- Want to track progress and celebrate achievements

**Customer motivation:**
- Desire to become a citizen and contribute to their new country
- Want to feel prepared and confident on test day
- Appreciate tools that respect their culture, language, and journey

## 4. Features (Must Accurately Describe)

### 4.1 Multi-Country Support
- **US Naturalization** — ~800,000 tests/year, ~10–15% failure rate
- **Canada** — ~250,000 tests/year, ~20–30% failure rate
- **UK** — ~170,000 tests/year, ~20–25% failure rate
- **Australia** — ~150,000 tests/year
- **France** — ~110,000 tests/year
- **Spain** — ~120,000 tests/year

### 4.2 Languages
- **App language:** English, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese
- **Quick Language:** Optional second language to swap to during quizzes when reading difficulty arises (e.g., test in English, swap to Spanish for help)

### 4.3 Study Modes
| Mode | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Random Quiz Mode** | Practice with questions in random order |
| **Flash Card Mode** | Flip through questions in order. Tap to show or hide answers |
| **Sprint Quiz** | Answer a small set in a short timed session (with countdown) |

### 4.4 Clever Antony (AI Assistant)
- **Name:** Clever Antony
- **Role:** Search assistant for exam questions
- **Behavior:** User types a topic or question (e.g., "Supreme law of the land", "Declaration of Independence"); Antony returns matching questions from the official question bank with official answers
- **Scope:** Works across all 6 supported countries; suggestions are contextual to the user's selected exam

### 4.5 Stats & Progress
- Quizzes completed
- Days active
- Average score
- Longest streak
- Most quizzes in a day
- Quiz history with calendar view

### 4.6 Share & Celebrate
- Share achievements after completing a quiz
- Message: "I just took a citizenship quiz and answered X out of Y questions correctly!"
- Encourages spreading progress to motivate others

### 4.7 Personalization
- Profile photo
- Change target country
- Early grading toggle (end quiz early and get grade for answered questions only)
- Terms accessible from profile

- **Friendly, approachable** — Rounded corners, soft borders

## 7. Copy Guidelines

- **Tone:** Warm, respectful, inclusive, encouraging
- **Avoid:** Jargon, hype, fear-based messaging
- **Emphasize:** "Come together," "blur borders," "your culture and talents," "education"
- **Do not:** Overpromise or claim guarantees (e.g., "Pass your test!")

## 8. Technical Notes for Implementation

- **Platform:** Web (HTML/CSS/JS or React/Next.js)
