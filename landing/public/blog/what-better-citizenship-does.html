What Better Citizenship Does

A guide to the app

Better Citizenship is a mobile study app that helps people prepare for citizenship tests in the US, Canada, UK, Australia, France, and Spain—in their preferred language with multiple study modes and an AI-powered search assistant.

6 Countries, Official Question Banks

We support citizenship and naturalization tests for six countries, each with its own official question bank:

  • United States — Naturalization test
  • Canada
  • United Kingdom
  • Australia
  • France
  • Spain

5 Languages

Study in English, Spanish, French, Hindi, or Chinese. We also offer Quick Language—an optional second language you can swap to during quizzes when you need help. For example, take the test in English and swap to Spanish when a question is difficult.

3 Study Modes

  • Random Quiz Mode — Practice with questions in random order
  • Flash Card Mode — Flip through questions in order; tap to show or hide answers
  • Sprint Quiz — Answer a small set of questions in a short timed session with a countdown

Clever Antony — AI Search Assistant

Clever Antony is our AI-powered search assistant for exam questions. Type a topic or phrase (e.g., "Supreme law of the land," "Declaration of Independence") and Antony returns matching questions from the official question bank with official answers. It works across all six countries and adapts to the exam you're preparing for.

Stats & Progress

Track your journey with quizzes completed, days active, average score, longest streak, and most quizzes in a day. View your quiz history in a calendar to see your study patterns.

Share & Celebrate

Share your achievements after completing a quiz. Spread the word—sharing your progress helps others stay motivated too.

Personalization

Add a profile photo, change your target country, and use the early grading toggle to end a quiz early and receive a grade for the questions you've answered. Terms are accessible from your profile.
